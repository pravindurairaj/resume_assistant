Muck Rack is the leading SaaS platform for public relations and communications professionals. Our mission is to enable organizations to build trust, tell their stories, and demonstrate the unique value of earned media. Muck Rack’s AI-powered, comprehensive, and integrated platform streamlines the PR workflow to help businesses generate positive media coverage, monitor mentions to manage brand reputation, and analyze PR’s impact on business outcomes. By combining media database, monitoring, and reporting into one dynamic platform, we empower teams to collaborate seamlessly, pitch effectively, and analyze results faster and more efficiently.
Founder-controlled, fully distributed, and growing sustainably, Muck Rack has received several awards for its unparalleled culture and product from organizations like Inc., Quartz, G2, and BuiltIn. We value resilience, transparency, ownership, and customer devotion, and infuse these values into everything we do.
We’re looking for a collaborative, self-motivated
Senior DevOps Engineer
to join our DevOps team.
As a Senior DevOps Engineer, you’ll help enable the broader engineering organization to do high-impact work by building and improving the infrastructure, tooling, and deployment systems they rely on every day. You’ll contribute to the development of standardized infrastructure and “paved paths” that make it easier to provision, deploy, and operate services reliably at scale.
You’ll collaborate closely with application engineering teams to ensure our container-based infrastructure is scalable, cost-efficient, and dependable across a wide range of workloads.
What You'll Do
Partner with DevOps and application engineering teams to design and improve standardized infrastructure, deployment workflows, and CI/CD pipelines
Build, operate, and continuously improve our containerized infrastructure, with a focus on reliability, scalability, and cost efficiency
Bring deep expertise in Kubernetes and its ecosystem, helping define best practices and support adoption across engineering teams
Manage and evolve cloud infrastructure on AWS, including compute, networking, and storage systems
Improve observability, monitoring, and incident readiness across production systems
Participate in a shared on-call rotation (approximately 1 week every 5 weeks), responding to and resolving production incidents
Mentor teammates and share knowledge to improve engineering practices and operational maturity across the organization
To Be Set Up for Success in This Role, You'll Need to Have
5+ years of experience in DevOps, infrastructure, or platform engineering roles
Strong hands-on experience with Kubernetes in production environments
Experience with infrastructure-as-code tools such as Terraform
Solid experience working with AWS (e.g., EKS, S3, IAM, RDS, VPC, Route53)
Experience building and maintaining CI/CD pipelines using tools such as GitHub Actions, CircleCI, Jenkins, or Argo CD
Experience working with containerized systems such as Docker and containerd
Familiarity with cloud security best practices and modern approaches to securing infrastructure, including shift-left principles
Experience operating and scaling high-traffic systems and large-scale data platforms in production environments
Strong problem-solving skills and a methodical approach to debugging and system design
Ability to communicate clearly with both technical and non-technical stakeholders in a distributed environment
If Any of the Below Also Describe You, This Could Be an Exciting Opportunity

Interest in continuously evaluating and adopting new technologies where they provide clear value
