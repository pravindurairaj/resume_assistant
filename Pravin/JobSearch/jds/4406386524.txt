CREGG is currently looking for an MES Engineer in Dublin on a contractual basis.
The Manufacturing IT Engineer (MES) has the technical responsibility for the support and preventative maintenance of Manufacturing IT systems, primarily MES clients, printers, scanners and other hardware.
As a contract, the position offers excellent rates and the opportunity for a long-term placement
Roles and Responsibilities:
• Provide real-time front-line support to the production group on their manufacturing IT systems.
• Periodically applying approved patches using centralised patch management tools
• System Readiness Testing of production systems prior to handback for commercial manufacturing
• Periodic refresh of MES clients, printers, scanners, and other hardware as part of the preventative maintenance of these systems
• Reporting of progress to system owners and the wider IS team
• Regularly liaise with stakeholders to ensure effective communication on outages
• Adherence to GXP procedures on site in relation to all aspects of the position
A Valid EU work permit
Skills and Education:
• Third level qualification in Electrical, Electronic, Systems Engineering, Computer Science or Equivalent
• Min 2 years’ experience working with automation or IT systems, preferably in the Life Sciences/ Pharma sector
• Excellent communication skills
• Experience with Manufacturing Execution Systems such as Werum PAS-X. Zebra Printers, Barcode scanners etc an advantage
• Experience in managing system IT infrastructure is also an advantage
